About Moeen

Moeen Munir Ali OBE is an English international cricketer who plays for England in all formats of the game and currently representing KKR in franchise cricket. Born on 18 June 1987 in Birmingham, Moeen is a left-handed batsman and right-arm off-spin bowler.

He made his international debut in 2014 and has since become one of England's most valuable all-rounders, contributing significantly with both bat and ball across all formats of the game.

Test Cricket
